About Beauter

Beauter is a lightweight Web Designing framework developed with a focus on being ready to use and universal!. It's great for starting with your small projects, and is easy to customize. It is very plain and have small foot print, ideal for reducing loading times, and adding responsive capabilities.

History

Beauter was initially the stylesheet for the under development site, outboxcraft.com. But was so improved that it was easily integrated with other projects of Outboxcraft Labs. Hence the 'stylesheet' got an identity as 'Beauter'.

Author

Beauter was initially started by Shubham Ramdeo, he acts as the main maintainer of the project.

Launch

Beauter is still under development and experiments until version 1.0. Current BETA versions are for testing purposes and will go through a lot changes over time.

The Future

Beauter is being standardized and after that, templates and training materials will also be launched to provide professional services.

So stay connected :)
