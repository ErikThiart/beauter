Lists

You can add descriptive lists.

Coffee
Black hot drink
Milk
White cold drink
<dl>
  <dt>Coffee</dt>
  <dd>Black hot drink</dd>
  <dt>Milk</dt>
  <dd>White cold drink</dd>
</dl>

And the ordrered or unordered lists, nested with different styles.

  • A Computer
  • A Room
    1. A Computer
    2. A Room
    3. Internet
  • Internet
<ul>
<li>A Computer</li>
<li>A Room
  <ol>
   <li>A Computer</li>
   <li>A Room</li>
   <li>Internet</li>
  </ol>
</li>
<li>Internet</li>
</ul>
